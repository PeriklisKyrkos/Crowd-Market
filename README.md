# Crowd-Market
The objective of this project is the development of a collaborative system for the submission, search, and evaluation of supermarket consumer-goods offers among users.

The system allows its registered users to inform others about products available at a good price (according to their own criteria), operating alongside existing tools (such as e-katanalotis), which are updated only with regard to price information and not product availability, and which also do not include all supermarket stores (e.g., local chains and mini-markets).

The verification of offer validity is entrusted to other users, who have the ability to validate an offer (like), indicate that it is not valid (dislike), or report that the product is out of stock. Users can also indicate that an offer has ended.

To encourage participation, the system rewards users who contribute information with points.
